The program implements a form for collecting and structuring student data. Instead of the linear pack() manager, the grid() manager is used, allowing text labels and input fields to be aligned by rows and columns, creating a neat tabular layout. Clicking the "Submit" button initiates a validation algorithm: all three fields are checked for blank lines, and the age field is checked for integer values. If validation is successful, the data is formatted into a single text block and displayed on the screen, and the "Clear" button instantly clears the form for new entries.
